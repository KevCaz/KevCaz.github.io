Welcome to my personal website!

I am Kevin Cazelles,

a data scientist trained as a quantitative ecologist working as a consultant @inSileco Inc. (that I co-founded) and a data scientist for the Centre for Ecosystem Management.

Over the last 10 years I have been contributing to open source software, mainly by teaching how to use R for data science but also by contributing to R packages (see my GitHub profile). I blog about open source, data science and ecology see my latest blog posts!
