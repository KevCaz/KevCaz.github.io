Fill out your XFA forms with Master PDF

March 25, 2020
  pdf XFA forms

The other day I needed to fill out some XFA forms. On the website I was told to use Acrobat Reader 😨… which is not supported on Linux. So I looked for an alternative to work on Debian 10, when I stumbled into this answer on mentioning Master PDF, so I installed it:

1

$ apt-get install master-pdf-editor

and you know what? Worked like a charm! 🎆
